## Two Products, One Goal

### ShadowCorp SDK
**Query blockchain data and analytics**

Access blocks, transactions, accounts, tokens, validators, and DeFi positions. Execute JSON-RPC calls and Neo4j graph queries for forensic analysis.

Perfect for: Analytics platforms, explorers, compliance tools, trading bots.

**[Get Started →](/shadowcorp-sdk/installation)**

### NearConnect
**Connect wallets to your dApp**

Four-tier wallet architecture supporting web, extension, hardware (Ledger), and mobile wallets. Includes pre-built UI components and multi-layer security.

Perfect for: dApps, DeFi platforms, NFT marketplaces, games.

**[Get Started →](/nearconnect-installation)**
